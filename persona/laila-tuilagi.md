## Bio

Moana joined Contoso as a junior buyer after completing her Bachelor of Commerce at the University of Auckland. Over eight years she's worked her way up through vendor onboarding, strategic sourcing, and category management. She now leads procurement for Contoso's two largest spend categories — professional services and technology — managing a portfolio of 120+ active vendor contracts worth over $45M annually.

She's known across the organization for being thorough, data-driven, and impossible to rush past a missing clause. Her colleagues joke that she reads contracts the way other people read novels — cover to cover, with opinions.

## Responsibilities

- Manages end-to-end procurement for professional services and technology categories
- Negotiates master service agreements, statements of work, and renewals
- Owns vendor relationship management for 20+ strategic and preferred vendors
- Reviews contract terms against corporate procurement policy before legal handoff
- Tracks spend against budget and flags variance for finance review
- Coordinates with Legal, Compliance, and Finance on high-value or high-risk contracts

## Pain Points (Pre-Agent)

- Spends ~40% of her week on contract paperwork instead of strategic sourcing
- Manually cross-references procurement policy for every contract — the policy doc is 18 pages
- Has to chase Legal and Compliance separately for reviews, often waiting 2–3 weeks for signoff
- No easy way to benchmark terms against the existing portfolio ("Is 90-day payment terms normal for this vendor tier?")
- Duplicate data entry across ERP, Procurement Platform, and spreadsheets

## How She Uses the Agent Solution

| Agent | Moana's Use Case |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Contract Intake** | Submits new contracts for automated classification and urgency scoring — replaces her manual triage spreadsheet |
| **Legal Review** | Gets instant playbook-based review instead of waiting for Legal's queue |
| **Compliance & Risk** | Automated tax, insurance, and regulatory checks — catches issues she used to miss |
| **Portfolio Intelligence** | Benchmarks payment terms, liability caps, and vendor spend against historical data before negotiating |
| **Reporting** | Generates executive summaries for her VP instead of writing them manually |
| **Redline Tool** | Reviews tracked changes in Word with rationale comments — sends clean redlines to vendors |

## Demo Scenario

Moana receives a new $850K Master Service Agreement from **Meridian Consulting** for a 3-year enterprise transformation engagement. She submits it to the Contract Agent and within minutes has:

1. Classification as high-value MSA requiring VP approval
2. Legal review flagging a missing liability cap and non-standard indemnification
3. Compliance check showing insurance requirements aren't met for this contract value
4. Portfolio benchmark showing Meridian's payment terms are 30 days longer than the portfolio average for Strategic vendors
5. An executive summary ready for her VP
6. A redlined Word document with tracked changes and rationale comments to send back to Meridian
