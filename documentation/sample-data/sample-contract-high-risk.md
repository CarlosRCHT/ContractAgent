## RECITALS

Client requires IT consulting and digital transformation services. Consultant possesses expertise in IT strategy, systems integration, and digital transformation. The Parties wish to enter into this Agreement on the terms set forth below.

---

## 1. TERM AND RENEWAL

**1.1** This Agreement shall commence on February 1, 2024 and continue for an initial period of three (3) years.

**1.2** **This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive five (5)-year terms unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term.**

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Auto-renewal for 5-year terms violates AC-PROC-POL §4 (maximum 3-year auto-renewal). Prohibited term requiring EVP/CFO approval.**

---

## 2. SERVICES

**2.1** Consultant shall provide the following IT consulting and digital transformation services:

(a) IT Strategy Assessment and Digital Transformation Roadmap Development;

(b) Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system evaluation and implementation advisory;

(c) Legacy system modernization planning and execution support;

(d) Data analytics platform design and deployment;

(e) Change management and training services;

(f) Project management office (PMO) services.

**2.2** Consultant shall assign a team of no fewer than eight (8) consultants to the engagement, with a designated Engagement Manager.

**2.3** Consultant may assign subcontractors at its discretion to perform any Services without prior approval from Client.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Unrestricted subcontracting without Contoso consent. Violates standard requirements per AC-PROC-POL §3 and Legal Playbook Item 14.**

---

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

**3.1** Client shall pay Consultant a monthly fee of One Hundred Thousand domestic Dollars (USD $100,000) for the Services, totaling One Million Two Hundred Thousand domestic Dollars (USD $1,200,000) annually.

**3.2** Consultant shall submit monthly invoices by the last business day of each month.

**3.3** **Client shall pay all invoices within one hundred twenty (120) days of the invoice date.**

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Net 120 payment terms. AC-PROC-POL §5 requires Net 60 for contracts $500K–$4.99M. Net 120 exceeds policy by 60 days. Non-standard — requires VP Finance approval.**

**3.4** Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of 1.5% per month (18% per annum).

**3.5** Consultant reserves the right to adjust fees annually upon thirty (30) days' written notice, reflecting market conditions and resource costs.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Unilateral price increase right. Prohibited per AC-PROC-POL §4. No CPI cap or limit on increases.**

---

## 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**4.1** Each Party represents that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement.

**4.2** Consultant warrants that Services will be performed in a manner generally consistent with industry practices.

**4.3** THE FOREGOING WARRANTY IS THE SOLE AND EXCLUSIVE WARRANTY PROVIDED BY CONSULTANT. ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY AND FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, ARE EXPRESSLY DISCLAIMED.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Broad warranty disclaimer. Minimal warranty language is weak compared to AC-PROC-POL standard. Missing warranties for compliance with laws, non-infringement, and anti-corruption.**

---

## 5. INDEMNIFICATION

**5.1** Each Party agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the other Party from claims arising from its own negligence.

**5.2** Client shall indemnify Consultant against any claims, damages, or losses arising from Client's use of the deliverables produced under this Agreement or Client's business operations.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Weak indemnification. Missing vendor indemnification for IP infringement, data breach, violation of laws, and willful misconduct. One-sided — Client (Contoso) indemnifies Consultant for use of deliverables. Does not meet AC-PROC-POL §3.1 requirements.**

---

## 6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**6.1** **CONSULTANT'S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY CLIENT IN THE THREE (3) MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM.**

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Liability cap = 3 months' fees = $300,000 on a $1.2M/year contract. This is 0.25x annual value — well below the AC-PROC-POL §3.2 minimum of 1x annual value (which would be $1.2M). The standard calls for 2x or $1M, whichever is greater ($2.4M).**

**6.2** **IN NO EVENT SHALL CONSULTANT BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, REGARDLESS OF THE CAUSE OF ACTION OR THEORY OF LIABILITY, INCLUDING BUT NOT LIMITED TO LOSS OF DATA, LOSS OF PROFITS, OR BUSINESS INTERRUPTION.**

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Blanket exclusion of consequential damages with no carve-outs. AC-PROC-POL §3.2 requires carve-outs for data breach and confidentiality breach.**

**6.3** **CLIENT SHALL BE LIABLE FOR ALL DAMAGES, WHETHER DIRECT, INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR OTHERWISE, ARISING FROM CLIENT'S BREACH OF THIS AGREEMENT, WITHOUT LIMITATION.**

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: UNLIMITED LIABILITY FOR Contoso. Strictly prohibited per AC-PROC-POL §4. This is a Major risk flag requiring General Counsel rejection.**

---

## 7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**7.1** All tools, methodologies, frameworks, templates, and know-how used or developed by Consultant in performing the Services, including any deliverables, shall remain the sole and exclusive property of Consultant.

**7.2** Client is granted a non-exclusive, non-transferable, limited license to use the deliverables solely for Client's internal business purposes during the term of this Agreement. This license terminates upon expiration or termination of the Agreement.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Vendor owns all deliverables including custom work product. License terminates with the Agreement. Violates AC-PROC-POL §3.3 which requires Contoso ownership of custom deliverables. Major risk — loss of Contoso assets.**

---

## 8. CONFIDENTIALITY

**8.1** Each Party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's proprietary information disclosed during the course of this Agreement.

**8.2** Confidentiality obligations shall continue for a period of one (1) year following termination.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Only 1-year survival period. AC-PROC-POL §3.4 requires 5-year survival (indefinite for trade secrets). Significantly below standard.**

**8.3** No provisions regarding return or destruction of confidential information.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Missing return/destruction obligation per AC-PROC-POL §3.4.**

---

## 9. TERMINATION

**9.1** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon ninety (90) days' written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within sixty (60) days of written notice.

**9.2** In the event of termination by Client for any reason other than Consultant's material breach, Client shall pay Consultant a termination fee equal to the remaining fees through the end of the then-current term.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: No termination for convenience. Contoso can only terminate for cause with a 60-day cure period. Plus, a punitive termination fee equal to the remaining contract value. Violates AC-PROC-POL §3.5. Major risk — operational lock-in.**

**9.3** No transition assistance provisions.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Missing transition assistance. Required for contracts > $250K per AC-PROC-POL §3.5 and Legal Playbook Item 21.**

---

## 10. DATA HANDLING

**10.1** Consultant shall use commercially reasonable measures to protect Client data.

**10.2** Client data may be stored and processed in Consultant's data centres located in the United States, India, or other locations as determined by Consultant from time to time.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Data may be stored outside the domestic jurisdiction (US, India, other). Violates AC-PROC-POL §7.1 (domestic-only data residency) and §3.7 (Data Privacy Regulation cross-border requirements). No CISO approval mechanism.**

**10.3** No specific data breach notification timeline or incident response provisions.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Missing breach notification requirements. AC-PROC-POL §7.1 requires 24-hour notification. Missing entirely.**

---

## 11. CYBERSECURITY

*This Agreement does not contain a cybersecurity requirements section.*

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: MISSING CYBERSECURITY REQUIREMENTS ENTIRELY. For a $1.2M IT consulting contract involving access to Contoso systems, this is a Major risk flag. AC-PROC-POL §7 requires SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, encryption standards, MFA, penetration testing, vulnerability management, and incident response.**

---

## 12. INSURANCE

*This Agreement does not contain insurance requirements.*

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: MISSING INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS ENTIRELY. AC-PROC-POL §6 requires for Professional/Consulting contracts: CGL ($2M/$5M), Professional Liability ($5M/$10M), Workers' Compensation (statutory), and Auto ($2M if applicable). None present.**

---

## 13. FORCE MAJEURE

*This Agreement does not contain a force majeure clause.*

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: MISSING FORCE MAJEURE CLAUSE. Required per AC-PROC-POL §3.6 and Legal Playbook Item 13. Moderate-to-Major risk, especially for a multi-year engagement.**

---

## 14. GOVERNING LAW

**14.1** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Delaware, United States of America.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Foreign governing law (Delaware, USA). AC-PROC-POL §3.8 requires New York State law. Foreign governing law requires General Counsel approval. Major risk flag.**

**14.2** Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in New York City, New York, under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Foreign arbitration (New York under AAA rules). AC-PROC-POL §3.8 requires AAA arbitration with the designated forum.**

---

## 15. GENERAL

**15.1** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties.

**15.2** Consultant may assign this Agreement to any affiliate or successor without Client's consent.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Vendor can assign without consent. Prohibited per AC-PROC-POL §4.**

**15.3** This Agreement may be amended by Consultant upon thirty (30) days' written notice to Client.

> ⚠️ **RISK FLAG: Unilateral amendment right. Consultant can modify the agreement without Contoso's consent. Extraordinary and unacceptable provision.**
