## RECITALS

WHEREAS Contoso requires operational equipment maintenance, facility maintenance, and related support services at various domestic facility locations; and

WHEREAS ProMaintain Services Inc. is a provider of equipment and facility maintenance services;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. TERM

**1.1** This Agreement commenced on July 1, 2021 and had an initial term of two (2) years, ending on June 30, 2023.

> [RISK FLAG: CONTRACT EXPIRED. The initial term ended June 30, 2023 -- over 12 months ago. There is no evidence of renewal or extension. Contoso may be operating under this agreement without a valid contract term, creating significant legal and operational risk.]

**1.2** This Agreement may be renewed for successive one (1)-year terms by mutual written agreement, provided that written notice of intent to renew is given at least sixty (60) days prior to expiration.

> [RISK FLAG: No renewal executed. The renewal mechanism requires mutual written agreement with 60-day notice, but there is no executed renewal amendment on file. If services are still being provided, this may constitute an implied contract with uncertain terms.]

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

**2.1** Provider shall provide the following maintenance services at Contoso's designated facility locations (Facility A, Facility B, Vancouver ):

(a) **Equipment Maintenance:** Preventive and corrective maintenance of forklifts, transport vehicles, industrial machinery, and other operational equipment;

(b) **Facility Maintenance:** HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and general facility maintenance for Contoso's terminal facilities, warehouse spaces, and operations centres;

(c) **Emergency Repair Services:** 24/7 emergency response for equipment failures and facility issues affecting service operations;

(d) **Seasonal Services:** Seasonal equipment preparation, maintenance scheduling, and readiness programs;

(e) **Regulatory Compliance Support:** Assistance with regulatory and facility authority compliance inspections.

**2.2** Provider shall maintain a minimum staffing level of twelve (12) qualified technicians across all three locations during operational hours.

---

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

**3.1** Contoso shall pay Provider an annual fee of Ninety-Five Thousand domestic Dollars (USD $95,000), payable in equal monthly instalments of USD $7,916.67.

**3.2** Emergency repair services outside of regular scope shall be invoiced separately at pre-agreed rates set forth in Schedule B (Rate Card).

**3.3** Contoso shall pay undisputed invoices within Net 30 days of receipt.

> [RISK FLAG: Payment terms do not match current policy. PROC-POL S5.1 (effective January 2024) specifies Net 45 for contracts valued $50K-$499K. This contract uses Net 30. While this benefits Contoso (shorter terms benefit the vendor, not the client), the contract should be updated to reflect current policy upon renewal.]

**3.4** Fees are subject to annual adjustment based on the Consumer Price Index, not to exceed 2% per year.

---

## 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**4.1** Each Party represents that it has the authority to enter into this Agreement.

**4.2** Provider represents and warrants that:

(a) All Services shall be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards for enterprise maintenance services;

(b) Provider shall comply with all applicable laws, including applicable industry regulations;

(c) Provider's technicians shall hold all required certifications and licenses;

(d) Provider shall comply with Contoso's safety policies and facility security requirements.

**4.3** Warranties shall survive for twelve (12) months following completion of Services.

> [RISK FLAG: Short warranty period. PROC-POL S5 template standard is 24 months. A 12-month warranty is below standard. Moderate concern.]

---

## 5. COMPLIANCE AND CERTIFICATIONS

**5.1** Provider warrants that it complies with the following standards:

(a) **industry regulatory authority requirements** for operational equipment maintenance -- Provider holds Approved Maintenance Organization certificate No. MO-2019-4567;

> [RISK FLAG: maintenance organization certificate may be expired. Certificate number references 2019 issuance. Maintenance organization certificates typically require periodic renewal. Current validity should be verified.]

(b) **domestic Standards Association (CSA) standards** for electrical and facility maintenance;

(c) **Industry Standards Body Safety Audit for Ground Operations (industry safety audit)** -- Provider certified as of March 2020;

> [RISK FLAG: Industry safety certification is from 2020. Industry safety registrations are valid for two years and must be renewed. A 2020 certification would have expired in 2022. This reference is outdated and Provider's current industry safety certification status must be verified.]

(d) **Occupational Health and Safety Act** compliance;

(e) **hazardous materials training standards** compliance for hazardous materials handling.

> [RISK FLAG: Reference to "hazardous materials training standards" is outdated. hazardous materials training was updated as part of the adoption of GHS (Globally Harmonized System). While the term "hazardous materials training standards" was the common name, references should be updated to reflect current Hazardous Products Regulations (HPR) under the Hazardous Products Act.]

## 6. INSURANCE

**6.1** Provider shall maintain:

(a) Commercial General Liability: $1,000,000 per occurrence / $2,000,000 aggregate;

> [RISK FLAG: INSURANCE MINIMUMS ARE OUTDATED. PROC-POL S6.1 (effective January 2024) requires $2M per occurrence / $5M aggregate for General Services. The current contract's $1M/$2M is significantly below the current policy minimum. For Enterprise Services (On-site), the requirement is even higher at $10M/$25M. Given that Provider performs on-site equipment maintenance, the higher standard may apply.]

(b) Workers' Compensation: As required by law;

(c) Automobile Liability: $1,000,000 combined single limit.

> [RISK FLAG: Automobile liability is below current standard. PROC-POL S6.1 requires $2M for General Services or $5M for Enterprise Services.]

**6.2** No requirement for Contoso to be named as additional insured.

> [RISK FLAG: Missing additional insured requirement. PROC-POL S6.2 requires Contoso to be named as additional insured on all CGL policies.]

**6.3** No requirement for Professional Liability insurance.

> [RISK FLAG: Missing Professional Liability / E&O coverage. For services involving regulatory compliance support and technical maintenance recommendations, Professional Liability coverage should be required.]

**8.2** Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.

> [Note: No carve-outs for excluded claims. Should include carve-outs per PROC-POL S3.2.]
