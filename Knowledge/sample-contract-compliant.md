## RECITALS

**WHEREAS** Contoso requires cloud infrastructure services to support its customer operations, loyalty program, and enterprise IT workloads; and

**WHEREAS** NovaTech Solutions Inc. is a provider of enterprise cloud infrastructure and managed services with demonstrated expertise in the enterprise sector;

**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. TERM

**1.1 Initial Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of three (3) years (the "**Initial Term**"), ending on February 28, 2027, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 13.

**1.2 Renewal.** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1)-year periods (each a "**Renewal Term**"), unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current Term. Automatic renewals shall not exceed a cumulative period of three (3) years beyond the Initial Term without express written agreement.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

**2.1** Provider shall provide the following cloud infrastructure services (the "**Services**"):

(a) **Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS):** Provision and management of compute, storage, and networking resources hosted in Provider's domestic data centres (primary and secondary locations);

(b) **Managed Services:** 24/7 infrastructure monitoring, patch management, incident response, and performance optimization;

(c) **Disaster Recovery:** Geo-redundant backup and disaster recovery services with recovery time objective (RTO) of 4 hours and recovery point objective (RPO) of 1 hour;

(d) **Migration Services:** Phased migration of Contoso's designated workloads from on-premises infrastructure, per the Migration Plan (Schedule A-2);

(e) **Security Services:** Managed firewall, intrusion detection/prevention, DDoS protection, vulnerability scanning, and security event monitoring.

**2.2** Detailed specifications, milestones, and acceptance criteria are set forth in Schedule A (Scope of Services).

**2.3** Provider shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry best practices for enterprise cloud service providers.

---

## 3. SERVICE LEVELS

**3.1** Provider shall meet the Service Level Agreement targets set forth in Schedule E, including:

| Service | SLA Target | Service Credit |
|---|---|---|
| Infrastructure Availability | 99.95% monthly uptime | 5% monthly fee credit per 0.1% below SLA |
| Incident Response (Critical) | 15 minutes | 2% monthly fee credit per 15-minute delay |
| Incident Response (High) | 1 hour | 1% monthly fee credit per hour delay |
| Incident Resolution (Critical) | 4 hours | 3% monthly fee credit per hour beyond SLA |
| Disaster Recovery RTO | 4 hours | Defined in DR Plan |
| Scheduled Maintenance Window | Sunday 02:00–06:00 ET | 48-hour advance notice required |

**3.2** Provider shall provide monthly SLA performance reports within ten (10) business days of each month-end.

**3.3** If Provider fails to meet any Critical SLA for three (3) consecutive months, Contoso may terminate this Agreement for cause.

## 11. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

**11.1** Provider shall comply with Data Privacy Regulation and Regional Privacy Regulation in processing Personal Information.

**11.2** Provider shall process Personal Information solely for performing the Services.

**11.3** Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures.

**11.4** All Contoso Data shall be stored and processed within the domestic jurisdiction — specifically in Provider's East Region and West Region data centres. No Contoso Data shall be transferred outside the domestic jurisdiction without CISO approval.

**11.5** Provider shall notify Contoso within twenty-four (24) hours of any actual or suspected data breach.

**11.6** Upon termination, Provider shall return or securely destroy all Personal Information per Section 10.5.

---

## 12. CYBERSECURITY REQUIREMENTS

**12.1** Provider shall maintain and comply with the following cybersecurity requirements:

(a) **SOC 2 Type II certification** — current report (Provider's most recent audit date: September 2023, Deloitte LLP);

(b) **ISO 27001:2022 certification** — Certificate No. IS-789456 (valid through August 2025);

(c) **Encryption:** AES-256 at rest; TLS 1.3 in transit;

(d) **Multi-factor authentication** for all personnel accessing Contoso systems and data;

(e) **Annual penetration testing** by independent third party (most recent: October 2023, CyberGuard Inc.), with results available to Contoso upon request;

(f) **Vulnerability remediation:** Critical within 72 hours; high-severity within 30 days;

(g) **Incident response plan** with notification to Contoso within 24 hours of any security incident;

(h) **Data residency:** All Contoso Data stored within the domestic jurisdiction (East Region and West Region data centres).

**12.2** Contoso may conduct security assessments with thirty (30) days' notice.

**12.3** Provider shall remediate identified deficiencies within mutually agreed timelines.

## 13. TERMINATION

**13.1 Termination for Convenience.** Contoso may terminate this Agreement at any time by providing sixty (60) days' prior written notice. Contoso shall pay for Services satisfactorily performed through the termination date.

**13.2 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the other Party materially breaches and fails to cure within the notice period.

**13.3 Immediate Termination.** Contoso may terminate immediately upon written notice for: (a) data breach or cybersecurity incident; (b) safety incident; (c) insolvency; (d) breach of anti-corruption or sanctions laws.

**13.4 Transition Assistance.** Upon termination, Provider shall provide transition assistance for ninety (90) days at then-current rates, including data migration, knowledge transfer, and operational handover documentation.

**13.5 Survival.** Sections 6, 7, 8, 10, 11, 14, and 15 survive termination.

## 15. FORCE MAJEURE

**15.3** Affected Party must: (a) notify within 5 business days; (b) mitigate; (c) resume promptly.
