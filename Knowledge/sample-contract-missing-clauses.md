## RECITALS

WHEREAS Contoso seeks to enhance its data analytics capabilities for operations optimization, customer experience improvement, and operational efficiency; and

WHEREAS SkyBridge Analytics Corp. is a domestic data analytics and artificial intelligence company specializing in enterprise data analytics;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## 1. TERM

**1.1** This Agreement shall commence on May 1, 2024 and continue for an initial term of two (2) years, ending on April 30, 2026.

**1.2** This Agreement may be renewed by mutual written agreement of both Parties for successive one (1)-year terms, with negotiations to commence no later than ninety (90) days prior to expiration.

---

## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

**2.1** Provider shall deliver the following analytics and data science services:

(a) **Operations Optimization Analytics:** Development and deployment of predictive models for regional profitability analysis, demand forecasting, and resource optimization;

(b) **Customer Experience Analytics:** Customer sentiment analysis, loyalty program optimization, and personalized service recommendation engines;

(c) **Operational Analytics:** Resource turnaround optimization, workforce scheduling analytics, and predictive maintenance alerting;

(d) **Dashboard and Reporting:** Executive dashboards, self-service analytics portals, and automated reporting pipelines;

(e) **Model Training and Maintenance:** Ongoing model retraining, performance monitoring, and accuracy optimization.

**2.2** Detailed specifications are set forth in Schedule A (Statement of Work).

---

## 3. FEES AND PAYMENT

**3.1** Contoso shall pay Provider a total annual fee of One Hundred Eighty Thousand domestic Dollars (USD $180,000), structured as follows:

| Component | Annual Fee |
|---|---|
| Operations Optimization Analytics | $60,000 |
| Customer Experience Analytics | $45,000 |
| Operational Analytics | $40,000 |
| Dashboard and Reporting | $20,000 |
| Model Training and Maintenance | $15,000 |
| **Total** | **$180,000** |

**3.2** Fees are payable in equal monthly instalments of USD $15,000.

**3.3** Contoso shall pay undisputed invoices within Net 45 days of receipt. [Per PROC-POL S5 -- contract value $50K-$499K]

**3.4** All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. Provider shall separately state Sales Tax (Registration No. 834567123RT0001) on all invoices.

**3.5** Fees shall remain fixed during the initial term.

---

## 4. SERVICE LEVELS

**4.1** Provider shall maintain the following service levels:

| Service | SLA Target |
|---|---|
| Analytics Platform Availability | 99.5% monthly uptime |
| Model Refresh Frequency | Weekly (or as specified per model) |

## 5. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

**5.1** Each Party represents that it is duly organized and has full authority to enter into this Agreement.

**5.2** Provider represents and warrants that:

(a) The Services shall be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards for data analytics providers;

(b) All Deliverables shall conform to agreed-upon specifications;

(c) Provider shall comply with all applicable domestic federal and state laws;

(d) The Services shall not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights;

(e) Provider has all required licenses and certifications;

(f) Provider shall comply with applicable anti-corruption laws including the applicable anti-corruption laws.

**5.3** Warranties shall survive for twenty-four (24) months following delivery.

---

## 6. INDEMNIFICATION

**6.1** Provider shall indemnify Contoso from claims arising from:

(a) Provider's breach of this Agreement;

(b) Provider's negligence or willful misconduct;

(c) Infringement of third-party intellectual property rights by the Services or Deliverables;

(d) Provider's violation of applicable laws.

**6.2** Contoso shall indemnify Provider from claims arising from Contoso's gross negligence or willful misconduct, subject to the limitation of liability.

**6.3** Standard indemnification procedures apply (notice, cooperation, control of defense).

---

## 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**7.1** NEITHER PARTY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY SHALL EXCEED THE GREATER OF: (A) TWO (2) TIMES THE FEES PAID IN THE TWELVE MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM; OR (B) ONE MILLION DOLLARS (USD $1,000,000).

[At $180K/year, the cap is: max(2 x $180,000, $1,000,000) = $1,000,000 -- compliant with PROC-POL S3.2]

**7.2** NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES, EXCEPT FOR: (A) BREACH OF CONFIDENTIALITY; (B) DATA BREACH; (C) GROSS NEGLIGENCE OR WILLFUL MISCONDUCT.

---

## 8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

**8.1** All Contoso data and materials remain Contoso's exclusive property.

**8.2** Deliverables and custom work product:

> "The ownership of intellectual property in custom models, algorithms, and analytics solutions developed under this Agreement shall be discussed and determined by the Parties on a case-by-case basis, taking into account the relative contributions of each Party."

> [RISK FLAG: Vague IP ownership language. PROC-POL S3.3 requires custom deliverables to be Contoso-owned as "work made for hire." This language creates ambiguity and potential disputes over who owns the analytics models built specifically for Contoso.]

**8.3** Provider retains ownership of its pre-existing analytics platform, tools, and frameworks, and grants Contoso a non-exclusive license to use them as part of the Services during the term of this Agreement.

> [RISK FLAG: License to pre-existing IP is only during the term. PROC-POL S3.3 requires a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license for pre-existing IP incorporated into deliverables.]

## 9. INSURANCE

**9.1** Provider shall maintain:

(a) Commercial General Liability: $2,000,000 per occurrence / $5,000,000 aggregate;

(b) Professional Liability (E&O): $5,000,000 per claim / $10,000,000 aggregate;

(c) Workers' Compensation: As required by law.

**9.2** Contoso shall be named as additional insured on CGL policies.

**9.3** Certificates of insurance to be provided annually.

[Note: Coverage levels are compliant with PROC-POL S6 for Professional/Consulting contract type.]

---

## 10. CONFIDENTIALITY

*This Agreement does not contain a confidentiality clause.*

> [RISK FLAG: MISSING CONFIDENTIALITY CLAUSE. This is a critical omission. Given that Provider will have access to Contoso's operations planning data, customer information, loyalty program data, workforce scheduling, and maintenance data -- all of which are highly sensitive -- the absence of confidentiality protections is a Major risk flag. Per PROC-POL S3.4 and Legal Playbook Item 16, a confidentiality clause is mandatory and cannot be omitted.]

---

## 11. DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

**11.1** Provider shall comply with Data Privacy Regulation and applicable state/regional privacy legislation in handling any personal information.

**11.2** Provider acknowledges that certain analytics work may involve customer personal information (booking data, loyalty profiles, travel patterns) and shall implement appropriate safeguards.

**11.3** All data processing shall occur within the domestic jurisdiction at Provider's primary and secondary facilities.

**11.4** Provider shall notify Contoso within twenty-four (24) hours of any data breach.

---

## 12. CYBERSECURITY

**12.1** Provider shall maintain:

(a) SOC 2 Type II certification (current report dated June 2023);

(b) Encryption of Contoso data using AES-256 at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit;

(c) Multi-factor authentication for all personnel accessing Contoso data;

(d) Annual penetration testing;

(e) Vulnerability remediation per industry standards.

**12.2** Contoso may conduct security assessments with reasonable notice.

[Note: Missing ISO 27001 requirement per PROC-POL S7.1, but SOC 2 Type II present. Moderate concern.]

---

## 13. TERMINATION

**13.1 Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate upon sixty (60) days' written notice if the other Party materially breaches and fails to cure within such period.

**13.2 Immediate Termination.** Contoso may terminate immediately for data breach, insolvency, or violation of applicable law.

**13.3** Upon termination, Provider shall return all Contoso data within thirty (30) days and certify destruction of copies.

*Note: This Agreement does not contain a termination for convenience clause.*

> [RISK FLAG: MISSING TERMINATION FOR CONVENIENCE. PROC-POL S3.5 requires Contoso to retain the right to terminate for convenience with 30-90 days' notice. Without this clause, Contoso can only exit the contract for cause, creating operational lock-in. This is an automatic Major risk trigger per Legal Playbook S3.2, Item 5.]

## 15. FORCE MAJEURE

**15.1** Neither Party shall be liable for failures due to events beyond reasonable control, including natural disasters, pandemics, government actions, war, terrorism, strikes, and transportation disruptions.

**15.2** Affected Party must notify the other within five (5) business days and mitigate.

**15.3** Either Party may terminate without penalty if Force Majeure continues for ninety (90) or more consecutive days.
